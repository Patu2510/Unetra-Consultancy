Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our partnership and referral programs.

To become a channel partner, simply choose the partnership tier that best suits your business needs and click on the "Apply Now" button. This will direct you to send an email to our partnerships team. We'll then reach out to discuss the details, verify your business information, and guide you through the onboarding process.

Commission rates vary based on the partnership tier and specific services. Silver partners typically earn 10-15% commission on successful placements, Gold partners earn 15-20%, and Platinum partners enjoy our premium 20-25% commission structure. Exact rates will be provided during your onboarding process and may vary based on position complexity and industry.

We typically establish partnerships for an initial period of 12 months, which allows enough time to develop a strong working relationship and see meaningful results. After this period, partnerships are automatically renewed unless either party provides notice. We believe in building long-term relationships that benefit both parties.

Our referral program rewards you for connecting us with qualified candidates. When you refer a candidate, include their resume and the position they're suitable for. If your referral is hired, you'll receive a bonus typically ranging from 5-10% of the candidate's first month's salary, paid within 30 days after the candidate completes their probation period.

All partners receive comprehensive onboarding training covering our recruitment processes, systems, and best practices. Gold and Platinum partners receive additional specialized training sessions and have access to dedicated support representatives. We also provide regular updates on industry trends, new recruitment methodologies, and periodic refresher training to ensure your success.
