Our Recruitment Process

At Unetra HR Solutions, we follow a structured and efficient recruitment process to ensure the best talent is matched with the right opportunities. Our workflow is designed to streamline hiring, making it seamless for both clients and candidates.

Our Recruitment Process

We follow a comprehensive and efficient process to ensure the best match between candidates and opportunities.

Resume Screening

We carefully analyze and shortlist resumes based on job requirements, ensuring candidates meet the necessary qualifications, skills, and experience.

Telephonic Interview & Evaluation

Shortlisted candidates undergo a detailed telephonic interview, where we assess their skills, experience, and suitability for the role based on client expectations.

Interview Scheduling

We coordinate and schedule interviews between clients and candidates, arranging virtual or face-to-face (F2F) meetings as per client preference.

Pre-Joining Formalities

Once selected, we assist candidates in completing pre-joining formalities, including document verification, background checks, and other necessary compliance requirements.
