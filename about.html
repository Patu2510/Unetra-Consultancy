About Unetra HR Solutions

Established in 2019 by a team of seasoned HR managers, Unetra HR Solutions has become a trusted name in recruitment and HR consulting, delivering excellence across multiple industries.

Trusted, 5+ Years Experienced & Specialists

With deep expertise in bulk hiring, lateral hiring, and leadership recruitment, we ensure that our clients receive well-screened, high-quality candidates suited to their organizational needs. Our strategic approach and commitment to excellence make us more than just a vendor—we function as an extension of your in-house HR team.

Our strength lies in providing high-quality candidates who not only meet job requirements but also align with your company's culture and values. We prioritize building long-term partnerships with both clients and candidates by offering personalized, tailored solutions.

At Unetra HR Solutions, we believe in giving back to the community. We pledge to share a portion of our profits to support meaningful causes, including:

  • Feed the Community programs for underprivileged individuals
  • Feed the Strays initiatives and dog food drives for shelters
  • Support for animal rescues and adopting shelters

Our Vision and Mission

At Unetra, our mission is to empower businesses by providing expert manpower consulting services that align with their strategic goals. We are committed to offering top-tier talent at the right time, helping organizations drive growth, innovation, and success with a skilled workforce. We aim to build long-term partnerships that enhance productivity, foster collaboration, and create sustainable value.

Our vision is to be the leading manpower consulting firm, trusted by businesses for high-quality, tailored staffing solutions that meet both short-term needs and long-term objectives. We aspire to be a catalyst for positive change in the workforce, where every placement contributes to building stronger, more resilient companies.
